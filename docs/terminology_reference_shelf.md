# Terminology Reference Shelf

These glossary meanings are original Sip Studies editorial definitions.
They were rewritten from scratch for professional beverage education and were not copied from the works below.

## Editorial Standard

- Definitions should read as working professional glossary entries, not study prompts.
- Definitions should stand alone without quoting copyrighted source prose.
- Source credit belongs in citations and shelf notes, not in the definition sentence itself.
